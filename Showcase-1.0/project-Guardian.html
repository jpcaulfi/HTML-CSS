Check Out  Guardian

Business Database

Guardian is the company database my team and I built during my time as a Data Analyst at Queen City Solutions in Buffalo, New York.

It houses all of the company's data, from insurance and payroll to inventory and depreciation. The intention was to implement a database system along with technology, such as tablets and mobile access, to drastically improve the company's financial performance through increased efficiency.

     

When the Guardian database was introduced, our team at Queen City Solutions was immediately able to identify numerous wasteful processes and inferior profit margins. In total, Queen City added an additional $20,000 to their bottom line in tax year 2018 as a direct result of the Guardian Database System.
