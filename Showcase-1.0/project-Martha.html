Check Out   'Stack-Em'

Fixed Income Investment Advisor

Stack-Em is an analytical tool designed to make planning for retirement easy. It assumes the individual is going to make routine monthly investments into fixed-income securities and also re-invest the cash flows from said securities.

The user simply inputs their current age, the age they want to retire, their retirement amount, and their monthly payment. Stack-Em does the rest.

   

Stack-Em calculates what your portfolio's equity would be, based solely on monthly investments without re-investing cash flows. Then it looks at the difference between your goal and that amount, and infers what necesary quarterly dividend you must recieve for the duration of your investment period in order to obtain your goal on time.

   

After all of that, the program scours the financial markets for potential candidates. Then, using industry-popular methods such as CAPM, correlation, and more, it builds a risk-averse portfolio and gives the user a few options.
