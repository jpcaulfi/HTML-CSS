Welcome to Jupiter Group

Finance & Tech Research Group

Jupiter Group is a research and collaborative group I formed after graduating college. I have been networking with individuals on LinkedIn, Facebook, etc. and also connecting with past peers of mine (school, work, friends, etc.) to grow the group's community.

The primary focus of the group is to discuss new developments in world markets and stay abreast of economic changes and their impact on global finance. We routinely discuss exciting content and have debates, encouraging different viewpoints and always trying to learn.

However, more recently, members have taken great responsibilities in pushing forward projects on artificial intelligence.

   

Individuals who join Jupiter Group are typically interested in understanding finance at the rich inner-industry level so that they may seek business opportunities. By mixing tech into the discussion, and recruiting primarily individuals with tech skills or experience, I aim to generate a virtual "think tank" where startups or side income can be created.

After all, it was discussion and research within Jupiter Group that fueled my algorithm trading bot projects...
